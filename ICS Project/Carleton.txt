Name of program: Interactive Multimedia and Design (IMD) Program
Entrance requirements: Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD), or equivalent, with a minimum of six 4U or 4M courses and includes the following courses: Advanced Functions (MHF4U)2, Five Additional credits, Portfolio
Length of program: Four years
Tuition: Living off campus: $18,535 – $23,215, Living on campus: $21,582 – $26,262
Credential(s) awarded: University Bachelor’s degree and advanced college diploma
